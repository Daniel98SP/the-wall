Side Scrolling Camera:
____________________________________________

Movement [W,A,S,D | ARROWS]
Look diagonal left [Q]
Look diagonal right [E]
Jump [SPACEBAR]
Shoot [M | Z]



Top-Down Camera:
____________________________________________

Movement [W,A,S,D |  ARROWS]
Move camera counter clockwise [Q]
Move camera clockwise [E]
Shoot [M | Z]
Slow down time for 5sex [X | N]
